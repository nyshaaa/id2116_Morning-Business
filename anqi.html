黄安琪

Born in Nanjing, China, raised in Singapore, she is currently studying her second year in Industrial Design in the National University of Singapore (NUS). She has been creating since she could hold a pen. At the age of five, she stared her first Art lesson. She incoperates Art and Science in her creations. She has a keen eye for interesting framings and forms and loves exploring the human body through taking portraits and figures. Right now, she's currently working on 4 projects: MORNING BUSINESS with Nysha, LAMP, BAR EXPERIENCE when Virtual meets Reality and WOOD.
